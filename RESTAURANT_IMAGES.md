# Restaurant Image URLs

To get the actual images for each restaurant, you can:

1. **Google Maps**: Search for each restaurant name + location on Google Maps, then right-click on their main photo and "Copy image address"
2. **TripAdvisor**: Visit each restaurant's TripAdvisor page and get image URLs
3. **Social Media**: Check Facebook/Instagram pages for high-quality images
4. **Official Websites**: Many restaurants have galleries on their websites

Once you have the image URLs, update them in `app/data/restaurants.ts` in the `imageUrl` field for each restaurant.
